Zhengwei Tao

Boya Postdoctoral Fellow | Peking University

I am a Boya Postdoctoral Fellow at Peking University, working on agent training, evaluation, and data synthesis in close collaboration with Prof. Wentao Zhang. I also serve as an advisor at UniPat.ai, where I study how agents can forecast future events, including forecasting model evaluation, training, trading, and commercialization.

I received my B.S. from Zhejiang University and my M.S. and Ph.D. from Peking University, advised by Prof. Zhi Jin.

My long-term research interests include event forecasting and agent data synthesis. I welcome discussions and collaborations, and I am continually recruiting research interns. Please feel free to reach out; I am always happy to connect.
